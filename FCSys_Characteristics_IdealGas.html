FCSys.Characteristics.IdealGas

Ideal gas

Information

This package contains the pressure-volume-temperature relationship of an ideal gas. Thermal data (e.g., heat capacity) is not included.

Extends from BaseClasses.CharacteristicEOS (Base thermodynamic package with only the p-v-T relations).
