FCSys.Quantities

Quantities to represent physical properties

Information

Here, the unit attribute of each Real variable actually denotes the dimension. The dimensions are noted in terms of angle (A), length (l), mass (m), amount or particle number (N) and time (T). Capital "L" and "M" are not used to abbreviate length and mass because they are not recognized in Dymola 7.4. In FCSys, temperature and charge are considered to be derived dimensions (see the Units package).

Extends from Modelica.Icons.Package (Icon for standard packages).
